## Routes (brief)

- **Anger:** Acknowledge heat; others' acts vs your judgment; life is short for petty anger.
- **Grief:** Do not rush; reason trims excess grief, not all grief; what can still be honored.
- **Insult:** If true, improve; if false, their error; often indifference and return to your work.
- **Social / pursuit / indirect contact:** Acknowledge 鬱悶 or 無奈. Not in control: their response, directness, feelings. In control: express intent once with dignity, how long to wait, not catastrophizing silence. No generic dating advice. One concrete step; one gentle question.
